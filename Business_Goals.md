## About Us

**Company Name:** NovaMind Tech Solutions
**Industry:** AI Consulting & Digital Automation
**Founded:** 2024

NovaMind helps South Asian SMEs move from manual, spreadsheet-driven operations to intelligent, automated workflows — powered by open-source AI, custom agents, and ERP integrations. We specialize in making enterprise-grade automation accessible to businesses with 5–100 employees.

## Target Audience

- **Primary:** SME founders and operations managers (10–100 employees) frustrated with manual data entry, slow invoicing, or inconsistent customer communication
- **Secondary:** Freelance developers and tech teams wanting to upskill in AI agent development
- **Demographics:** Entrepreneurs aged 28–50, Pakistan/South Asia, digitally literate but not technical, annual revenue $50K–$2M

## Value Proposition

Why do customers choose us?

1. **Done-for-you AI systems** — we build and deploy, not just consult
2. **Local market expertise** — we understand South Asian SME constraints (cost, internet, team size)
3. **Training included** — clients own their system; no vendor lock-in

## Key Differentiators

- We work with open-source tools (no $500/month SaaS dependencies)
- All automations are fully audited and HITL-controlled — clients stay in control
- Flat-fee project pricing; no hidden hourly billing
